Saturn.

Saturn is probably the most iconic planet ever. With its distinctful ring system, which were first observed by Galileo Galilei in 1610. Since the rings already look pretty amazing from Earth, NASA sent out PIONEER 11 which returned the first closeup images of Saturn.

The Ring System

Saturn's rings are composed mostly of ice particles which range from a few centimeters to kilmetres across. The ring system is divided into 14 sections.

The rings of Saturn as imaged by VOYAGER 2 in 1981.
