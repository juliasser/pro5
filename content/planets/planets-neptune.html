Neptune!

Neptune is the most distant planet from the sun. It's the smaller one of all ice giants, and — as you can imagine — it's pretty damn cold out there. Come take a look!

Climate!

It's not only icy, it's pretty damn windy on Neptune. Winds can reach about 600 m/s — in 1989, NASA even observed a storm as big as Earth, which lasted several years.

Neptune's Great Dark Spot, a storm as huge as planet Earth.
